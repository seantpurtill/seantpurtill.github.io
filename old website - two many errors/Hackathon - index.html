Are you LOST?

if you are lost or with someone who has a beeping IAMLOST tracker:

  1. Remain Calm
  2. Check Tracker to see if your caretaker has alerted you. If there is a blinking blue light, help is on the way
  3. Contact the number on the back.
  4. Contact Police if possible.
  5. DO NOT LEAVE WHERE YOU ARE, UNLESS IT IS UNSAFE
  6. DO NOT LEAVE WITH A STRANGER

IS SOMEONE YOU CARE FOR LOST?

If you are a caretaker of someone with a IAMLOST Tracker, and they have alerted you that they are lost:

    Turn Alert on.


  1. Alert the tracker that you are coming to find them, by selecting the command above.
  2. Return to the last place you were together.
  3. IF you cannot find your loved one, Contact Police.

About the IAMLOST Tracker

  1. The IAMLOST Tracker is designed to help caregivers find their loved one.
  2. The Tracker is built into a small communicator that is connected to the TELUS network, and allows caregivers to send alerts to the tracker, and allow their ward to let the caregiver know when they are lost.

  3. If the IAMLOST tracker is pressed five times:

    1. An alert is sent to the caretaker.

    2. The Caregiver can then message back that they are on the way.

    3. The tracker will then beep, and the owner may need to stay in place and so his or her caregiver find them. This also makes it easier for the police to locate the missing person if necessary.
